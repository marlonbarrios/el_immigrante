El Immigrante

This web application—Cuento Eterno—uses Luis Felipe Lomelí's microcuento "The Emigrant" as the generative seed for an ever-unfolding digital narrative.

"—Forgetting something?
—If only."

By feeding this tiny story into a suite of large language models (LLMs) and custom GPTs, the app continuously spins new "chapters" on the theme of exile, memory, and loss.

Each generated fragment becomes part of an endless conversation across languages and cultures, exploring the universal experience of displacement and remembrance.
